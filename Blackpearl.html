Granite information

  • Family: Igneous Rock
  • Formed: Slow crystallization of molten magma within the Earth
  • Distinctive features: Rich Dark Tones with Pearlescent Flecks
  • Color: Predominantly black with reflective silver or blue pearlescent flecks.
  • Texture and granularity: Fine to medium-grain texture with a smooth and polished finish, contributing to its luxurious appearance.
  • Location: Telangana & Karnataka, India
  • Strengths & Weaknesses: Durable, Luxurious Appearance, Versatility, Variation in Appearance, Maintanance.
  • Applications: Countertops and Vanity Tops, Flooring, Wall Cladding, Monuments and Memorials, Decorative Elements
